What is Spelllbook

Spelllbook is a collection of 10,000 NFTs of different Spellls.
Each Spelll has a unique five-letter word and a combination of 26 possible traits. Spelll is also your access key to the Spelllbook Chamber, a members-only club with benefits, perks, and a solve-to-earn puzzle game.
Members will use the power of letters and words on the Spellls to solve puzzles and earn ETH, Tokens, and NFTs.

10k Unique NFT Spellls

Each Spelll is unique with a different five-letter word and a combination of 26 possible traits.

Smart Contract

Standard ERC-721 contract backed by IPFS file storage and registered on the Ethereum Blockchain.

Fair Distribution

Buying a Spelll costs 0.05 ETH. No bonded curve. The minting is random and only 10k Spellls will ever be minted.

MEMBERS-ONLY

Access key to the Spelllbook Chamber, a members-only club with benefits, perks and rewards.

SOLVE-TO-EARN PUZZLE GAME

A story-driven puzzle game, exclusivly for the members. Members will use the power of the words and letters of the Spellls to solve escape room like puzzles and earn rewards.
